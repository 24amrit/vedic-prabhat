    Vedic prabhat centre, Utarpradesh

    Its unique energy, structure, and location make the Isha Yoga Center a tremendously powerful space for selftransformation. – Maharaja shri

    To emphasize eternal values of surrender, faith, and resilience in navigating life’s challenges as exemplified by Shri RamBhakt ‘Bharat’, ‘Divya Jyoti Jagrati Sansthan’ organized a mesmerizing theatrical play titled ‘अंति म परी क्षा ’on 24th January 2025 as part of the Natyotsava-theatrical exclusive at Mahakumbh, Prayagraj (Uttar Pradesh). This grand event was an integral part of the ‘Divya Jyoti Chalchitram Series 2’, conceptualized under the spiritual guidance of ‘His Holiness Ashutosh Maharaj Ji’. The play featured exceptional performances by self-less volunteers of DJJS, who captured the emotional andspiritual nuances of the saga with great depth. The event brought together hundreds of devotees and seekers from across the globe, uniting them in a shared experience of divine inspiration.
