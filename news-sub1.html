The Legacy of Maharaj Shri

A Beacon of Spiritual Wisdom Explore Maharaj Shri’s journey as a spiritual luminary and his impact on seekers worldwide.

15/02/25

spiritual-others

Read in Hindi

To emphasize eternal values of surrender, faith, and resilience in navigating life’s challenges as exemplified by Shri Ram Bhakt ‘Bharat’, ‘Divya Jyoti Jagrati Sansthan’ organized a mesmerizing theatrical play titled ‘अंति म परी क्षा ’on 24th January 2025 as part of the Natyotsava-theatrical exclusive at Mahakumbh, Prayagraj (Uttar Pradesh). This grand event was an integral part of the ‘Divya Jyoti Chalchitram Series 2’, conceptualized under the spiritual guidance of ‘His Holiness Ashutosh Maharaj Ji’. The play featured exceptional performances by self-less volunteers of DJJS, who captured the emotional and spiritual nuances of the saga with great depth. The event brought together hundreds of devotees and seekers fromacross the globe, uniting them in a shared experience of divine inspiration.
